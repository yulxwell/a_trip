Discover Korea's Top Attractions

South Korea offers a fascinating mix of ancient tradition and cutting-edge modernity. From royal palaces that have stood for centuries to the bustling, neon-lit streets of its megacities, there is something for every type of traveler. This guide will introduce you to some of the most iconic and beloved attractions across the country, providing a starting point for your incredible journey.

Gyeongbokgung Palace, Seoul

As the largest and arguably most beautiful of Seoul's five grand palaces, Gyeongbokgung is a must-see for any visitor. Built in 1395, it was the main royal palace of the Joseon Dynasty. Though much of it was destroyed during various invasions, it has been meticulously restored to its former glory. Spend a few hours wandering through its vast grounds, admiring the intricate architecture, and don't miss the impressive Royal Guard Changing Ceremony at the main gate. Renting a 'hanbok' (traditional Korean clothing) to wear while you explore adds to the immersive experience.

Busan's Coastal Charm

For a different vibe from Seoul, head to the southern port city of Busan. Known for its beautiful beaches, fresh seafood, and laid-back atmosphere, Busan is a favorite destination for locals and tourists alike. Haeundae Beach is famous for its wide sandy shore and upscale hotels, while Gwangalli Beach offers stunning nighttime views of the Gwangan Bridge. For a more cultural experience, visit the colorful Gamcheon Culture Village, a vibrant community of houses built in staircase-fashion on the foothills of a coastal mountain.

Jeju Island: A Volcanic Paradise

A short flight from the mainland, Jeju Island is Korea's largest island and a designated UNESCO World Natural Heritage site. It's a paradise for nature lovers, boasting dramatic volcanic landscapes, stunning waterfalls, and unique lava tube caves. Hike up Hallasan, a dormant volcano and South Korea's highest peak, or watch the sunrise from Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak). The island is also famous for its pristine beaches and quirky museums, making it a perfect getaway for relaxation and adventure.
