About This Site

Welcome to Korea Visit, your trusted partner for an authentic Korean travel experience. This site was born from a passion for sharing the true culture of Korea and a desire to help travelers create unforgettable memories.

Our Philosophy

We believe that travel is about more than just seeing the sights. It's about connecting with a new culture, understanding its people, and discovering hidden gems that don't always make it into the guidebooks. Our content is carefully curated to provide you with practical, insightful, and reliable information that helps you do just that.

Our Founder

This site is run by a dedicated individual with a deep love for Korean culture. With two years of experience as an Airbnb host, I have had the privilege of welcoming guests from all over the world and sharing my local knowledge to enhance their trips. Those interactions inspired the creation of this website—a platform to offer personalized advice and answer your specific travel questions, just as I would for a friend. We aim to be your trusted local contact, ensuring you have the support you need to explore Korea with confidence.

Thank you for visiting, and we look forward to helping you plan your amazing journey!
